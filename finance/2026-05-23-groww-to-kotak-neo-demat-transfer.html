Transfer equity holdings from Groww to Kotak Neo without selling

A digital-first checklist for moving shares directly from one demat account to another using CDSL Easiest / broker-supported demat transfer routes, while preserving holdings instead of selling and rebuying.

Verdict: First confirm whether Kotak Neo is CDSL or NSDL. If both Groww and Kotak are CDSL, use CDSL Easiest with Kotak added as a trusted account. If Kotak is NSDL, ask both brokers for the supported inter-depository route before initiating.
Best caseCDSL → CDSL

Mostly digital via CDSL Easiest

Register on CDSL Easiest with the Groww demat BO ID, add Kotak Neo as trusted beneficiary, then transfer each holding by ISIN and quantity.

Timelinepractical

3–7 working days end-to-end

Registration/beneficiary approval usually takes 1–3 working days. The actual securities transfer often completes same day to 2 working days after successful submission.

Taxsame PAN

No sale if it is your own demat

For same-owner transfers, it is generally not a capital-gains event. Keep buy dates, contract notes, and transfer confirmations so Kotak and tax records remain correct.

Recommended path

  1. Get both demat details. From Groww, note BO ID and depository. From Kotak Neo, note depository, BO ID or DP ID + Client ID.
  2. Check if Kotak is CDSL or NSDL. CDSL accounts use a 16-digit numeric BO ID. NSDL accounts usually have a DP ID starting with IN plus Client ID.
  3. If Kotak is CDSL: register at CDSL Easiest, add Kotak as trusted account, wait for approval, then transfer holdings ISIN-wise.
  4. If Kotak is NSDL: ask Kotak/Groww whether online CDSL-to-NSDL inter-depository transfer is supported for your account. If not, use their DIS / digitally assisted DIS route.
  5. Transfer only settled and unpledged holdings. Avoid shares pending settlement, pledged shares, and securities near messy corporate action dates unless you track them carefully.
  6. After credit in Kotak: verify quantities, import/update acquisition price if needed, and save evidence for taxes.

Detailed checklist

Before starting

  • Download Groww holdings statement.
  • Download Groww trade/contract notes for original acquisition cost.
  • Confirm Kotak demat is active and KYC/PAN matches.
  • List each ISIN and quantity to transfer.

CDSL Easiest setup

  • Go to CDSL Easiest from CDSL’s site.
  • Register using your Groww CDSL BO ID.
  • Complete OTP/TPIN/authentication.
  • Wait for DP/CDSL activation if required.

Beneficiary setup

  • Add Kotak Neo demat as trusted beneficiary if it is CDSL.
  • Verify exact BO ID; a wrong BO ID can send shares to the wrong account.
  • Wait for trusted-account approval/cooling period.

Transfer submission

  • Select Groww source demat and Kotak destination.
  • Enter ISIN and quantity for each stock.
  • Use own-account/off-market/self-transfer reason where available.
  • Authenticate and save confirmation.

Charges and tax notes

Charges: Expect Groww/CDSL/DP debit charges, commonly per ISIN or per instruction. Check Groww’s latest DP tariff before executing; receiving shares at Kotak is often free but should still be confirmed.

Tax: A transfer between your own demat accounts under the same PAN is generally not a sale. Your original purchase date and cost should carry forward. Kotak may not automatically know your old cost basis, so manually fix/import acquisition values if the platform allows.

Records to preserve: Groww holdings before transfer, contract notes, CDSL transfer acknowledgement, Kotak holdings after transfer, and any broker support emails.

Messages to send brokers

Kotak Neo support
I want to transfer my existing equity holdings from my Groww demat account to Kotak Neo without selling them.

Please confirm:
1. Is my Kotak Neo demat account with CDSL or NSDL?
2. What is my full BO ID / DP ID + Client ID for receiving shares?
3. If my source account is Groww CDSL, can I add Kotak Neo as a beneficiary/trusted account in CDSL Easiest?
4. If Kotak is NSDL, do you support CDSL-to-NSDL inter-depository transfer digitally, or do I need a DIS slip?
5. Are there any inward transfer charges or specific instructions I should follow?
Groww support
I want to transfer my equity holdings from Groww demat to my own Kotak Neo demat account without selling.

Please confirm:
1. My Groww demat BO ID and depository.
2. Whether my account is enabled for CDSL Easiest.
3. Whether I can transfer to a Kotak Neo demat digitally through CDSL Easiest.
4. Charges for off-market transfer, including per ISIN / per instruction charges.
5. Cut-off time and expected processing time.
6. Process if target Kotak demat is NSDL instead of CDSL.
