Source: https://blog.quantinsti.com/trading-strategy-52-weeks-high-effect-in-stocks/

Rulebook

Market fit: India-direct / India-adapted from QuantInsti NSE article
Instrument/universe: local cached NIFTY50 stock daily OHLC basket
Data required: daily OHLC for stocks; rolling 252-session high; monthly rebalance calendar.
Signal rule: at first trading day of each month, select stocks whose prior close is within 5% of the prior 252-session high (close >= 0.95 × 252-day high).
Entry timing: buy selected stocks equal-weight at that month’s first session open.
Exit timing: hold for the month; square off/rebalance at next month’s first session open.
Sizing/risk: equal-weight active names; no leverage; cash if no names pass.
Costs/slippage: 5 bps on turnover.
Why systematic: monthly measurable price-to-52-week-high rule; no discretionary selection.
