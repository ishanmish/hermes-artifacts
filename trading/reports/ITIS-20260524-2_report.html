ITIS-20260524-2 — Faber 10-month SMA trend-following timing, adapted to NIFTY

Source: https://mebfaber.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/SSRN-id962461.pdf

Rulebook

Market fit: India-adaptable
Instrument/universe: NIFTY 50 index daily OHLC proxy
Data required: monthly closes / daily opens; 10-month SMA (implemented as month-end close > trailing 10-month SMA).
Signal rule: risk-on when prior month-end close is above its 10-month SMA; risk-off otherwise.
Entry timing: enter at next available session open for the following month after risk-on signal.
Exit timing: exit at next available session open for the following month after risk-off signal.
Sizing/risk: 100% NIFTY exposure when risk-on, cash when risk-off; no leverage.
Costs/slippage: 5 bps on allocation changes.
Why systematic: public moving-average timing model with fixed monthly rule.
