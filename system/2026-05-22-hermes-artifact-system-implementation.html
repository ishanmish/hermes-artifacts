Chosen template mapping

Use the Anthropic gallery as a format library. The publisher does not force one universal layout; the artifact author picks the nearest example and adapts content into that structure.

research

14 · research feature explainer

Best for travel research, market explainers, Reddit synthesis, concept notes, and source-backed investigations.

planning

16 · implementation plan

Best for workflow/system changes, project plans, phased execution, and status of what changed.

operations

11 / 12 · status or incident report

Best for recurring summaries, cron failures, ops reports, and anything that needs a crisp executive update.
